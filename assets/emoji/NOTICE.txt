Keynope Emoji Glyphs

The coloured block glyphs in keynope-emoji-glyphs.bin.gz are modified
derivatives of Noto Emoji artwork, Copyright 2013 Google LLC.

The source artwork was converted into Keynope's maximum-size 80 by 40
terminal-cell representation. Each stored cell contains a Unicode quadrant
mask and an RGB colour. Keynope samples this canonical representation when
rendering smaller emoji sizes. The original PNG artwork is not distributed.
